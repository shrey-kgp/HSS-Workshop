Accomodation

Accommodation: We have double bedded AC and non ac rooms. So if you wish to stay single it will cost you 400/ per day and if you wish to share your room with someone you shall pay 300/ per day. On the other hand, if you wish to stay single in NON AC it will cost you 250/ and if you stay with someone you shall have to pay 150/

Payment: Payment shall be made through debit cards. Participants are requested to get their ATM Debit Cards

Food: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner are available in the Guest House. There are around 7-8 eateries and restaurants in the campus

Travel: Participants are requested to take care of their own Travel, accommodation and food
